About Us

Founded in 2014, the Chayah Kalahari Project NPC is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rewilding Kalahari meerkats threatened by the illegal pet trade and habitat loss. With a proven 90% release success rate, we have returned nearly 4,000 meerkats to the wild, safeguarding the ecological integrity of the Kalahari desert ecosystem.
